# Reviewer Guide: Cost Control Video Seminar

Welcome to the interactive preview of the **Cost Control Video Seminar**. This application uses AI to help you navigate and learn from Lin Pardey's cruising wisdom.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. The Video File (Required)
**Crucial:** To protect copyright and ensure high-performance playback, this app **does not stream the video from the cloud.** You must have a local copy of the "Cost Control" video file on your computer.

1. Open the application URL.
2. Click the **"Select Video File"** button in the center of the player.
3. Choose the mp4/mkv file of the Lin Pardey seminar from your hard drive.
4. The app will immediately link the video to the interactive transcript and AI.

### 2. Permissions
When prompted, please **Allow Microphone Access**. This enables the "Listening" feature so you can talk directly to the Bosun AI.

*   **Tip:** If you are viewing this app within the AI Studio frame and the microphone doesn't seem to respond, click the **"Open in new tab"** button (top right of the preview) for the most stable experience. Browsers are often more permissive with audio features in a direct tab than in an iframe.

---

## ⚓ Key Features to Test

### The Bosun AI Assistant
The "Bosun" is your guide. Located at the bottom of the screen, you can either **type** your questions or click the **Microphone** icon to speak.

*   **Try asking:** "What does Lin say about fuel costs?" or "How do I choose the right boat?"
*   **Contextual Jumping:** When the Bosun answers, look for a **"Jump to Video"** button in the chat bubble. Clicking it will automatically seek the video to the specific timestamp discussed.

### Musical Interludes
For a bit of entertainment between lessons:
*   **Query:** Ask the Bosun to **"play some music"** or **"start a musical interlude."**
*   **Behavior:** The Bosun will acknowledge the request, and the video will automatically jump to one of the relaxing musical segments in the seminar.

### Voice & Listening
*   **Bosun Voice:** Toggle the "Voice" switch to have the Bosun speak his replies out loud. If you don't hear anything at first, toggle it **OFF and back ON** to "prime" the browser's audio engine.
*   **Listening Mode:** Click the "Listening" microphone to speak your queries. The waveform at the bottom shows your voice activity. Note: Browsers require a user gesture (like this click) to enable audio.

### Manual Navigation
*   **Sidebar:** Open the sidebar (top-left) to see the full list of chapters.
*   **Progress Tracking:** As the video plays, watch the chapter list highlight your current position.

## 🔒 Privacy & Performance
*   **Local Processing:** Your video file stays on your computer. It is never uploaded to any server.
*   **Browser Compatibility:** For the best experience (including high-quality voice synthesis), we recommend using **Google Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge**.
